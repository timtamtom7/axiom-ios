## 50-Word Description

Axiom is a private belief audit app built on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy principles. Add your beliefs, collect supporting and contradicting evidence, and run AI-powered stress tests to discover which beliefs hold up — and which need revision. Everything stays on your device.

---

## 100-Word Description

Most of us walk around carrying beliefs we never consciously chose — "I'm not good enough," "Mistakes are intolerable," "I don't deserve success." Axiom helps you find them, examine them, and test them against real evidence.

Built on decades of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy research, Axiom lets you log your beliefs, track supporting and contradicting evidence, and run AI stress tests that reveal weak links in your thinking. The result: a clearer picture of what you actually believe, and what you might want to change. Privacy-first: all data stays on your device.

## App Store Description (Full — EN)

### Short Description
Examine, challenge, and strengthen your core beliefs with evidence-based CBT techniques. Private. Local. Yours.

### Promotional Text
**Your beliefs shape everything.**
The job you don't apply for. The relationship you don't pursue. The voice that says you're not enough. Axiom helps you find the beliefs driving your life — and puts them to the test.

**Add a belief.**
Start with anything: "I am not good enough," "Mistakes are intolerable," "I deserve love." Axiom maps it as a central node.

**Collect the evidence.**
Add what's been said, done, or felt that supports or contradicts this belief. Green for supporting. Red for contradicting.

**Run an AI Stress Test.**
Axiom's AI examines your evidence network, identifies the weakest connection, and asks: what if this evidence doesn't mean what you think it means?

**See your score adjust.**
Watch your belief's confidence score shift as you challenge its weakest links.

**Your mind, your data.**
All belief data is stored locally on your device. Your thoughts are yours — we never see, sell, or share them.
