# Axiom App Preview Video — 30 Seconds

## Overview

A 30-second App Store preview video for Axiom. Tone: calm, cerebral, slightly mysterious — like watching a mind organize itself. No voiceover. Ambient, minimal sound design. Dark aesthetic throughout.

**Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical (for iPhone App Store), with 16:9 and 4:5 variants for iPad and App Store search ads.

**Frame rate:** 60 fps (for smooth motion on network graph animations)

**Color palette:** `#0a0a0f` background, `#e879f9` accent for belief nodes, `#4ade80` for supporting evidence, `#ef4444` for contradicting evidence, `#ffffff` for text.

---

## Scene 1 — Opening (0:00–0:05)

**Duration:** 5 seconds

### Visual
- Screen fades in from pure black (`#000000`)
- Single glowing node appears center-screen — soft purple (`#e879f9`) with a pulsing halo
- The node slowly rotates, revealing it is a belief structure with a faint score ring
- Subtle particle dust drifts in the void

### Audio
- Low, warm ambient drone (E minor, 80 BPM feel)
- Single soft chime as node appears

### Text
- Fade in at 1s: **"You are what you believe"**
- Font: New York, bold, white
- Centered, lowercase
- Fades out at 4s

### Overlay
- Axiom wordmark bottom-left: "axiom" in SF Pro, light weight, `#8B8B9B`

**Duration:** 7 seconds

### Text / UI Labels

- Button: `+`
- Text field placeholder: "Enter a belief…"

## Scene 3 — Evidence (0:12–0:20)

**Duration:** 8 seconds

### Visual

### Audio

### Text / UI Labels

## Scene 4 — AI Challenge (0:20–0:27)

**Duration:** 7 seconds

### Visual
- **"AI Challenge"** button (SF Symbol `brain`) glows and is tapped
- Screen transitions to AI Stress Test results
- The weakest edge (the "My boss said my work was 'fine'" node) pulses amber and is highlighted
- AI commentary text fades in:
  - **"This evidence is subjective."**
  - **"'Fine' is not a measure of worth."**
- Score ring animates: 78% → **65%** (with a subtle downward motion)
- Difference label: "−13%" appears in amber
- The contradicting evidence node pulses brighter

### Audio
- AI activation: a brief digital "processing" tone (200ms)
- Weak link highlight: a single note bends downward
- Score decrease: a descending tone (subtle, not dramatic)

### Text / UI Labels
- Header: "AI Stress Test"
- AI Insight: "This evidence is subjective. 'Fine' is not a measure of worth — it's a neutral descriptor. Challenge this link."
- Score: "78% → 65%"
- Adjustment: "−13% after analysis"

## Variant Notes

### iPhone (9:16)
Primary delivery. Full 30 seconds. Use the exact timeline above.

### iPad (4:5)
Crop to center-frame on the UI. Same duration. UI elements scale to larger viewport.

### App Store Search Ad (16:9)
Landscape cut. Same scenes, reframed. Scene 1: node + text left/right composition. Scenes 2–4: UI centered, dark bars top/bottom. Scene 5: tagline + logo left, badge right.
