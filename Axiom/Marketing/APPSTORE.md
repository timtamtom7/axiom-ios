## Description

**You can't think your way out of anxious thoughts — but you can learn to respond to them differently.**

Axiom brings the principles of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) into your daily life. It's not about positive thinking. It's about developing a deeper understanding of how your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors interact — and using that understanding to create meaningful change.

**What you'll find inside:**

- **Thought Records** — A structured tool for examining anxious or distorted thinking patterns. Identify the situation, explore the thoughts, challenge the cognition, and track your progress over time.

- **CBT Psychoeducation** — Grounded in established psychological frameworks. Learn how cognitive distortions work, how avoidance maintains anxiety, and how behavioral experiments can break the cycle.

- **Personalized Tracking** — Monitor your mood, thought patterns, and coping strategies over time. Understand what triggers you and what helps.

- **Compassionate, Clinical Tone** — No self-help clichés. No false promises. Just evidence-based tools presented with warmth and respect for your intelligence.

**Who is Axiom for?**

Axiom is designed for adults who want to work on anxiety, stress, and unhelpful thinking patterns — whether or not they're in formal therapy. It complements the work you might be doing with a therapist, or serves as a starting point for exploring CBT on your own.

**Important:** Axiom is not a replacement for professional mental health treatment. If you're in crisis, please contact a mental health professional or call emergency services.
