# Axiom 3.0 Vision Document

## Core Thesis
**Beliefs shape reality.** Every action, emotion, and outcome traces back to a belief held — often unconsciously. Axiom exists to make beliefs visible, testable, and changeable.

## Current State
Axiom 1.0: Personal belief audit tool
- 43,000 beliefs logged by early users
- Average belief score change: -12 points (toward healthier)
- Top belief category: self-worth

## 3.0 Innovation Areas

### 1. AI Therapist Augmentation
- AI therapist co-pilot during belief work sessions
- Real-time challenge suggestions during evidence entry
- Predictive belief drift alerts

### 2. Belief Genome Project
- Map common human beliefs across cultures/ages
- Benchmark your beliefs against anonymized cohorts
- Identify universal cognitive distortions

### 3. Preventive Belief Work
- Detect belief formation triggers in real-time
- Intervene before harmful beliefs solidify
- Positive belief inoculation

### 4. Clinical Integration
- HIPAA-compliant therapist portal
- Treatment plan alignment with CBT protocols
- Outcomes data for clinical validation
