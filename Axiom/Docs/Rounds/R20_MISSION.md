# Axiom 10-Year Mission

## Mission
Democratize Cognitive Behavioral Therapy through software. Every person with a smartphone deserves access to the same belief-work tools that therapists use with clients — at a fraction of the cost.

## Why This Matters
- 1 in 4 people experience a mental health episode annually
- Therapy is expensive ($150+/hour) and inaccessible
- CBT is the gold standard treatment — but tools are fragmented
- No app has won this space despite massive need

## 10-Year Goals
1. **10M users** by 2034
2. **$100M ARR** by 2034
3. **Clinical validation** published in peer-reviewed journal
4. **FDA clearance** as digital therapeutic
5. **Partnerships** with 3 major health insurers for reimbursement
6. **Open-source core** of belief work framework

## Personal Legacy
Tommaso built Axiom because he used CBT to change his own beliefs about himself. The belief "I'm not smart enough" held him back for 20 years. After 6 months of systematic belief work, it shifted. He wants this for everyone.

## Values
1. Privacy first — your mind is yours
2. Clinical rigor — we don't make stuff up
3. Radical simplicity — 10 seconds to start
4. No gamification — this isn't a game
