## Description

**Axiom** is a thoughtful CBT-powered journaling app that helps you examine, challenge, and reframe the beliefs that shape your daily life.

### Core Features

- **Belief Auditing** — Record beliefs, rate your confidence (0–100%), and identify cognitive distortions with guided prompts
- **Evidence Tracking** — Log supporting and contradicting evidence for each belief to build a balanced perspective
- **Guided Exercises** — Work through structured CBT exercises including cognitive reframing, thought records, and behavioral activation
- **Community Insights** — Browse anonymized patterns from the community to feel less alone in your struggles
- **Progress Tracking** — Maintain a daily streak and track how your belief confidence shifts over time

### Who Is It For?

Axiom is for anyone curious about their own thought patterns — whether you're working with a therapist, exploring self-improvement, or simply want a clearer mind.

### Therapeutic Philosophy

Axiom does not diagnose or treat mental health conditions. It is a journaling companion inspired by CBT principles, not a substitute for professional care.
