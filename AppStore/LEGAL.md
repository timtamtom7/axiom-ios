## Privacy Policy Summary (for App Store)

**Data Collection:** Axiom stores all belief and evidence data locally on your device. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal data.

**iCloud Sync:** If enabled, your data syncs across your devices via your personal iCloud account. Apple encrypts all data in transit and at rest.

**Analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking.

**Health Information:** If you choose to connect Apple Health, health data is never transmitted to our servers — it stays on-device.

**Children:** Axiom is not intended for users under 12.

**Mental Health Disclaimer:** Axiom is not a licensed mental health service. It is a self-guided tool. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent any mental health condition.
