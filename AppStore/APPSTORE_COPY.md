## Full Description (3500 chars)

**You are what you believe.**

Every emotion you feel, every action you take, every relationship you have — it all traces back to a belief you hold about yourself, others, or the world. Most of these beliefs formed without your consent, shaped by experience, culture, and chance. Some serve you. Some don't.

Axiom is a belief audit tool that helps you examine your beliefs systematically, challenge them with evidence, and strengthen the ones that actually serve you.

**How it works:**

1. **Add a belief** — Write down a thought you hold about yourself or the world. Rate how strongly you believe it from 0-100%.

2. **Collect evidence** — Add moments when this belief was clearly true, and moments when it was clearly false.

3. **Get AI-challenged** — Axiom's AI identifies your belief's weakest points and asks the questions that matter most.

4. **Watch it shift** — Your score changes as your evidence base grows. Over time, you build a clearer, more accurate belief system.

**What makes Axiom different:**

- Evidence-based CBT techniques, adapted for self-guided use
- AI that challenges without judgment
- No gamification — this is real inner work
- Your data stays private, on your device
- Belief network visualization shows how your beliefs connect
- Community sharing lets you anonymously contribute to collective belief research

**Who it's for:**

- People in therapy who want to do belief work between sessions
- People who can't afford therapy but want CBT tools
- Anyone curious about the beliefs shaping their reality
- Curious people who want to stress-test their thinking
